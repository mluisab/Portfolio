IS&P Process


Instructions

To ease the execution of the IS&P assessment, these instructions offer a clear guideline and summary of the necessary steps. It is indicated to follow this guide in preparation, and after completing the IS&P process to ensure the success of this procedure. For further support, check out the IS&P checklist which provides additional details to facilitate you during this process.

*If personal data are processed*

  1. Make sure you have completed the preparation part of the checklist before starting the actual IS&P risk assessment. This includes contacting necessary stakeholders and arranging a meeting to complete the assessment. The meeting should be planned in the early stages to allow enough time for the entire procedure. Within the meeting, the following stakeholders should be present:
    • System manager (you)
    • Operational manager
    • Security Specialist
    • Privacy Specialist

  2. During the workshop, discuss the following parts of the form:
    • IS Questionnaires (sections 1.2 to 1.4)
    • Privacy Questionnaire (section 1.5)

  3. After completing the questionnares, determine:
    • The IS risk profile (Low, Medium, High)
    • Which (if any) additional IS measures are required
    • Whether a DPIA should be conducted
    • Which (if any) additional privacy measures are required

  4. After the workshop, make sure you complete the follow-up steps included in the checklist such as writing a report summarizing the results and sending it to essential stakeholders.
