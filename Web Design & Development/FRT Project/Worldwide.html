Facial Recognition Worldwide

Facial Recognition Status

This map shows the current status of facial recognition use in multiple countries, highlighting the differences between them.










As of 2020, 99 countries have implemented facial recognition tehcnology and 7 in 10 governments are using this technology for mass surveillance. However, 2 countries have banned the use of facial recongition, specifically Belgium and Luxembourg.




Country Scores

Researching the use of facial recognition across different countries, a ranking has been created to determine how widespread and invasive certain countries are. Each country was assesed out of 40, where the low socres indicated high level of facial recognition invasive use and high scores showed a less invasive use. The topics on which the countries were scored on include transportaion, government use, police, airports or schools.
